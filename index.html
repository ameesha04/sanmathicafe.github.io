SANMATHI CAFE-YOUR DESTINATION FOR RELAXATION

At Sanmathi Cafe, we are on a mission to provide you with a unique and inviting space where you can escape the stresses of daily life and embark on a journey of relaxation and culinary delight.

  • Our cafe is more than just a place to eat
  • it's a sanctuary of serenity, a haven for savoring delectable food, and a retreat for rejuvenating your senses.
  • Sanmathi Cafe is designed to be your destination for relaxation. Whether you're sipping a carefully crafted cappuccino, savoring a delicious meal, or simply enjoying the soothing background music, every moment at our cafe is meant to be an experience.

Our vision is simple yet profound: to create a space where people can slow down, unwind, and savor the moment. In today's fast-paced world, we understand the importance of finding moments of respite. Our cafe is designed to offer you that much-needed escape. We've meticulously crafted an ambiance that is both tranquil and inviting, a place where you can forget your worries and focus on enjoying the present.

Sizzling Brownie

Sizzling hot brownie is a delightful dessert that combines the warmth of a freshly baked brownie with the sizzle of a hot platter, creating a tantalizing experience for your taste buds.

The rich, gooey brownie paired with the sizzling ice cream and decadent chocolate sauce is a heavenly treat that never fails to satisfy your sweet cravings and leaves you with a contented smile.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani, a culinary masterpiece, is a symphony of flavors that never fails to captivate the senses. The aromatic blend of fragrant basmati rice, tender chicken, and a rich medley of spices creates a tapestry of taste that's simply irresistible

What truly sets Chicken Biryani apart is its ability to bring people together. It's more than just a dish; it's a celebration on a plate. Whether shared with loved ones at a festive gathering or savored as a comforting meal at home, the tantalizing aroma and exquisite taste of Chicken Biryani have the power to create cherished memories. Its versatility and universal appeal make it a timeless classic, and a testament to the culinary artistry of Indian cuisine.

Dal Kolhapuri

Dal Kolhapuri is a culinary masterpiece that hails from the heart of Maharashtra, India. This rich and flavorful lentil dish embodies the essence of traditional Indian cuisine, making it a delightful addition to any meal.

What sets Dal Kolhapuri apart is its bold combination of aromatic spices and a medley of lentils, resulting in a truly unique and mouthwatering taste. The dish's hearty texture and robust flavors make it a perfect choice for both vegetarians and those seeking a satisfying, protein-rich meal.

Chicken Schezwan Noodles

Chicken Schezwan Noodles are a delectable fusion of flavors. The tender, succulent chicken pieces mingle with the spicy and aromatic Schezwan sauce, creating a tantalizing harmony. The noodles themselves provide a satisfying base, soaking up the bold flavors and adding a delightful texture to every bite.

What makes this dish truly outstanding is its versatility. It caters to both spice enthusiasts and those seeking a flavorful kick in their meal. The perfect balance of heat and umami is a culinary masterpiece that leaves your taste buds dancing. Whether you're enjoying it as a quick takeout treat or as a comforting homemade meal, Chicken Schezwan Noodles are a delightful and irresistible indulgence.

Veg/Non Veg Pizza

Pizza is the ultimate comfort food, a timeless classic that unites people worldwide. Its irresistible combination of a crispy, doughy crust, savory tomato sauce, and melted cheese is a culinary masterpiece.

Furthermore, it's the perfect choice for gatherings, bringing friends and family together to share slices, laughter, and cherished memories. The aroma of a freshly baked pizza is enough to make anyone's mouth water, promising a delightful, satisfying meal every time. Pizza transcends cultural boundaries, making it a universally beloved dish that has stood the test of time.
